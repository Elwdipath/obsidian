# Full Body Routine Overview

This routine includes **3 full-body workouts per week** (A, B, C). Each day targets all major muscle groups with compound lifts and accessory movements. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

## 🏋️ Workout A

**Focus:** Upper chest, quads, back, hamstrings, arms.

| Exercise | Sets | Reps |
|----------|------|------|
| Incline Dumbbell Press | 3 | 8–12 |
| Barbell Squats | 3 | 6–8 |
| Chest-Supported Dumbbell Row | 3 | 8–12 |
| Seated/Standard Leg Curl | 3 | 10–12 |
| Superset (Optional)* | 3 | — |

\* *Finisher or superset options might include accessory arm work (e.g., curls + extensions) — depending on video continuation.* :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

---

## 🏋️ Workout B

**Focus:** Strength emphasis with pressing, hinging, pulling and unilateral work.

| Exercise | Sets | Reps |
|----------|------|------|
| Barbell Bench Press | 3 | 4–6 |
| Romanian Deadlifts | 3 | 6–8 |
| Wide-Grip Lat Pulldowns | 3 | 8–12 |
| Walking Lunges | 3 | 6–10 per leg |
| Behind-the-Back Cable Lateral Raise + Reverse Crunches (Superset) | 3 | — |

> *Exercises in Workout B are from the companion written routine associated with the same training philosophy and creator.* :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

---

## 🏋️ Workout C

**Focus:** Shoulders, back, glutes, legs, calves.

| Exercise | Sets | Reps |
|----------|------|------|
| Dumbbell Shoulder Press | 3 | 8–12 |
| One-Arm Dumbbell Row | 3 | 8–12 |
| Hip Thrusts | 3 | 10–15 |
| Leg Extensions | 3 | 10–15 |
| Cable Flyes | 3 | 10–15 |
| Standing Calf Raises + Cable Rear Delt Flyes (Superset) | 3 | — |

> *Workout C also comes from the full 3-day rotation commonly shared alongside the video.* :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

---

## 📅 Weekly Template

| Day | Workout |
|-----|---------|
| Monday | Workout A |
| Wednesday | Workout B |
| Friday | Workout C |

Repeat each week, resting 1 day between sessions. Adjust weights so that the last reps are challenging but form stays strict.

---

### Notes

- **Warm-up:** Always begin with 5–10 min of light cardio & dynamic mobility.
- **Progression:** Increase weight or reps gradually each week.
- **Rest:** ~2 min between heavy sets; ~60–90 sec for accessory movements.
