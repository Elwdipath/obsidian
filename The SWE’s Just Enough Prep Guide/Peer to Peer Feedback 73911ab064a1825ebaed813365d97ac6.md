# Peer to Peer Feedback

<aside>
<img src="https://www.notion.so/icons/verified_blue.svg" alt="https://www.notion.so/icons/verified_blue.svg" width="40px" /> **Congratulations, you’ve made it through the Just Enough Prep Guide!**

You should now have a revised resume and a thorough Github profile. You should also be prepared to pass the initial interview and technical screens with defined processes for each. 

If nothing else, you should feel noticeably more confident in your technical communication skills.

**Now that you have a solid foundation**, use this template to get feedback from peers. Alternately, join an [Underdog.io](http://Underdog.io) cohort and shoot us a quick message at community@underdog.io to get a lightweight review of your hireability.

</aside>

<aside>
<img src="https://www.notion.so/icons/document_blue.svg" alt="https://www.notion.so/icons/document_blue.svg" width="40px" /> **Resume** — Does the resume tell a story? Does it demonstrate business value? Score the following resume items on a scale of 1-5. Offer feedback for anything below a 3.

| **Does your resume…** | **Score (1-5)** |
| --- | --- |
| Demonstrate that you delivered business value |            **0** |
| Focus on quantitative metrics |            **0** |
| Display a broad & deep knowledge of specific technologies |            **0** |
| Show that you work well with other engineers |            **0** |
| Exhibit empathy for stakeholders & teammates |            **0** |
| Display professionalism |            **0** |
| Contain enough tangible ‘hooks’ to have a solid conversation |            **0**  |
| **Total** |            **0** |

</aside>
