# The SWE’s Just Enough Prep Guide

# 👋 Welcome!

This guide is intended to help you become job search ready.

Our assumption is that you’re a mid-level to senior-level software professional planning to re-enter the job market. You’ve been doing this for years, and you’re good at what you do. You just need a little polish to make yourself job search ready.

We have put together a practical and actionable **Just Enough Prep Guide** to help you communicate your value in a way hiring managers understand. By the end of this guide, you’ll have the resources to become an engineering candidate that knows how to showcase your skills and interview effectively.

We’re here if you have any questions.

– The [Underdog.io](http://Underdog.io) Team

# What you need to know:

---

<aside>
1️⃣ **How long will this take?**

That’s entirely up to you. Most people work through it in a few hours but you may need more or less time depending on your working style. 

Skim the guide, ctrl+F for the answers you’re looking for, take what you need, and leave the rest.

</aside>

<aside>
2️⃣ **Who designed this course?**

Tim Mansfield was a tech lead at Google and YouTube for several years and has long experience in roles ranging from senior engineer to CTO at various startups. He has also pursued a parallel track in education, being on the founding team of a charter high school, starting an education non-profit, helping run multiple coding bootcamps, and designing new-hire and up-skilling training for many companies.

One of his major motivations is to help people succeed over the course of their careers, and he has mentored hundreds of people with that in mind.

</aside>
