## ğŸ³ Instructions
1. Heat oil and 1 tbsp butter in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Cook the seasoned chicken until done. Remove and set aside.
3. Add remaining 4 tbsp butter to the pot.
4. Add onion, carrot, and celery. Season with salt and pepper. Cook for 5 minutes.
5. Add garlic and cook for about 1 minute.
6. Stir in flour and cook for 1â€“2 minutes.
7. Slowly stir in chicken broth and heavy cream.
8. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and herbs de Provence.
9. Bring to a boil.
10. Add broccoli and broken lasagna sheets.
11. Reduce heat and simmer ~15 minutes until pasta is tender.
12. Stir in Parmesan cheese.
13. Add the cooked chicken back to the pot.
14. Stir well and season with salt and pepper as needed.
15. Serve hot and enjoy.
