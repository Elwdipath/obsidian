# Chicken Shawarma

## 🛒 Ingredients

### 🐔 Chicken Marinade
- 3 lbs chicken thighs
- 1/2 cup plain yogurt
- 5 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp coriander powder
- 1 tbsp cumin powder
- 1 tsp cardamom powder
- 2 tsp ground fenugreek
- 1 tbsp white vinegar
- 1 tbsp red chili powder
- 1 tbsp onion powder
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- Juice of 1 lemon (about 2 tbsp)
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp black pepper

---

### 🥒 Yogurt Sauce
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 3/4 cup grated cucumber (salted & squeezed dry)
- 2 medium garlic cloves, salted & pureed
- 1/2 cup finely chopped cilantro
- 1/2 tsp coriander seed (ground or crushed)
- 1/2 tsp cumin seed (ground or crushed)
- Juice of 1/2 lemon

---

### 🫓 For Serving
- Flatbreads
- Thinly sliced red onion
- Salted cherry or grape tomatoes
- Crunchy lettuce
- Yogurt sauce
- Spicy sauce (optional)

---

## 🔪 Instructions

### 1️⃣ Marinate the Chicken
1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, garlic, spices, vinegar, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Mix until smooth.
3. Add chicken thighs and coat thoroughly.
4. Cover and refrigerate **at least 2 hours**, ideally overnight.

---

### 2️⃣ Cook
**Grill Method (Preferred)**
- Preheat grill to medium-high.
- Grill chicken 5–7 minutes per side until charred and internal temp reaches **165°F**.
- Rest 5–10 minutes before slicing thin.

**Oven Method**
- Bake at 425°F for 20–25 minutes.
- Optional: broil 2–4 minutes for char.
- Rest before slicing.

---

### 3️⃣ Make Yogurt Sauce
1. Salt grated cucumber and squeeze excess liquid out.
2. Combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl.
3. Chill 30 minutes before serving.

---

### 4️⃣ Assemble
- Warm flatbread.
- Add sliced chicken.
- Top with lettuce, onion, tomatoes.
- Drizzle yogurt sauce and spicy sauce.
- Wrap tightly and serve.

---

## 🔥 Notes
- Chicken thighs give the best shawarma texture and juiciness.
- Fenugreek gives authentic depth — don’t skip it.
- For extra char flavor, slice and sear chicken in a hot pan for 1–2 minutes after grilling.
- Great for meal prep — keeps 4 days in fridge.

---

## 🧊 Storage
- Chicken: 4 days refrigerated.
- Sauce: 3–4 days refrigerated.
- Freezes well (without sauce).
