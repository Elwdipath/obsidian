### In system design exercises, why is it acceptable for different people to produce different diagrams or solutions for the same

It's acceptable because initial requirements are often vague and people have dirfferent perspectivves and experiences that inform their approach. As log as the solution makes logical sense and can be explained, and the end goal is achieved (like the customer getting their pizza), the design is valid.

### What are key skills involved in system design of a business process #systemdesign #flashcards

Breaking down requirements into logical steps, thinking critically about what needs to be accomplished, and identifying what details are necessary versus what can be abstracted away. This involves asking the right questions to clarify requirements before designing the system.

### When a product manager asks you to design a system, what level of detail should they typically care about? #systemdesign #flashcards 
Product managers typically care about features being build, requirements, accessibility, supported languages, and high-level architectural decisions. They usually don't care about implementation details like whether typescript or javascript is used.
